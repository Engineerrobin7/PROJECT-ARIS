## üí° PRO TIPS

1. **Be Natural** - Speak/type naturally, ARIS understands variations
2. **Keywords Matter** - Include action words (open, search, play, etc.)
3. **Be Specific** - "Weather in Paris" vs just "Weather"
4. **Try Variations** - Multiple ways to say the same thing work
5. **Check Logs** - See `logs/aris.log` for debugging
